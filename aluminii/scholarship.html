Introduction

Scholarships are financial aids provided to students to support their education.
They come in various forms to cater to different needs and goals.
Explore the types below to learn more about how you can contribute.

Types of Scholarships

  • Merit-Based Scholarships

    Awarded based on academic achievements, extracurricular activities, or other talents.

  • Need-Based Scholarships

    Provided to students based on their financial need.

  • Minority Scholarships

    Designed to support students from underrepresented groups.

  • Disability Scholarships

    For students with disabilities or chronic illnesses.

  • Athletic Scholarships

    Awarded based on athletic talent and performance.
